Goal/s

Our project aimed to enhance student enrollment by developing a visually appealing and user-friendly website that effectively communicated important information. The primary objective was to create a platform that would raise awareness among parents and potential students in Singapore about the wide range of services offered by Baizonn, particularly in the subjects of mathematics and science.

Local Environments

Each team member utilized a local setup of Apache and MySQL using XAMPP as their individual environment for practicals and testing purposes. This familiar and consistent local environment allowed for seamless collaboration and ensured compatibility during the development process. As a precautionary measure, regular backups were taken before implementing any significant changes on the live site. Given the coordinated nature of our work and the preference for face-to-face meetings, a staging site was not required as most of the work was efficiently managed and monitored in real-time.
